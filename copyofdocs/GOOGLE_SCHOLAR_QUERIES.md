## How to Use These Queries

### Step 1: Open Google Scholar
Go to: https://scholar.google.com

### Step 2: Copy-Paste Each Query
- Paste exactly as shown
- Click "Search"

### Step 3: Filter Results
- Look for papers with "Computer Networks" in the title or venue
- Check publication year (2023-2025)
- Look for [PDF] or [HTML] links

### Step 4: Quick Screening
For each paper, check:
1. **Abstract:** Does it address similar problem?
2. **Results section:** Does it report numerical metrics?
3. **Comparison table:** Does it compare with other methods?

### Step 5: Download & Document
- Save PDFs to folder
- Fill in PAPER_DATA_COLLECTION.md template
- Note DOI for citation

## Tips for Better Results

1. **Use quotes** around exact phrases: "Computer Networks"
2. **Use OR** for alternatives: "edge" OR "mobile"
3. **Use year filters**: 2023 OR 2024 OR 2025
4. **Check citations**: Papers with 10+ citations are more credible
5. **Look at references**: Good papers cite relevant work
