🔗 SYSTEM INTEGRATION & WORKFLOW
===============================

main.py (CLI Orchestration): Unified entry point for setup, experiment, baseline, analysis, tests, and paper artifact generation. All algorithms run in a single, fair simulation environment with explicit node/step counts.

src/core/FedSemGNN.py: Central orchestrator for all algorithms and enhancement modules. Integrates online semantic learning, multi-cluster fault tolerance, extreme scale federation, hardware energy modeling, and testbed preparation.

src/algorithms/: All federated RL and baseline algorithms (FedSemGNN, FlatFedPPO, HierFedPPO, HSQF, RandomPlacement) implemented with standardized interfaces and metrics.

src/utils/: Semantic, graph, monitoring, and modeling utilities. Includes GNN encoders, semantic vector extraction, clustering, and instrumentation.

experiments/, visualizations/: All analysis, metrics, and visualization scripts. Automated generation of publication-ready graphs and tables.

results/, graphs/, logs/: All experiment results, metrics, logs, and visualizations are saved in standardized locations for reproducibility and publication.
