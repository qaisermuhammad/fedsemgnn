Essential Diagrams for High-Tier Journal Submission
System Architecture Diagram

Shows all major components: edge servers, users, base stations, cloud, communication links, and their interactions.
Highlights hierarchical, semantic, and federated layers.
Federated Learning Workflow Diagram

Step-by-step flow: data generation, local training, model aggregation, semantic adaptation, and feedback.
Shows both hierarchical and flat federation.
Mobility Model Diagram

Illustrates user movement, handover, and how mobility affects service placement and learning.
Semantic Matching & GNN Encoder Diagram

Visualizes the semantic vector extraction, GNN encoder, and projection head.
Shows how semantic similarity is computed and used for placement.
Fault Tolerance & Recovery Diagram

Depicts cluster health monitoring, failure detection, and automatic recovery mechanisms.
Hardware Energy Modeling Diagram

Shows different hardware profiles, DVFS, and energy measurement points.
Extreme Scale Federation Diagram

Illustrates clustering, communication compression, and scalability features for 10,000+ nodes.
Online Semantic Learning Flow

Shows real-time adaptation, feedback loop, and embedding updates.
Experiment Pipeline Diagram

End-to-end: dataset, simulation, metrics logging, analysis, and visualization.
Metrics & Results Flow

How metrics are collected, normalized, and compared across algorithms.
Deployment/Testbed Preparation Diagram

Shows how the system can be deployed on a physical testbed or cloud.
